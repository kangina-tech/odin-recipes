Lasagna Recipe

The thing about lasgna is that it has a way of bring people together - regardless of the time of year. So, today, we are going to share with you the world's best lasagna recipe (we really do not take this title lightly). That said, strap right in, get you utensils ready and let's get physical.

How to make Lasagna

Making lasgna can be time-consuming but the resuls are well worth it. You'll find a detailed ingredients list and step-by step instructions in the recipe below so let's start with the basics.

Lasagna Ingredients

While we do like the classic recipe, we'd be remiss if we didn't say that this is a very customizable list. Feel free to add more stuff - the more the merrier; someone once said.

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sause and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar to add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsely, dried basil leaves, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-biught to homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a very breif overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

The detailed layering instructions can be found in the recipe below but this is the order you will follow:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers then top with the remaining parmesan

How Long Does it Take to Cook?

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
