Peanut Butter Chicken Meatballs

These Thai-inspired peanut butter chicken meatballs are a quick appetizer, ready in under 30 minutes, using mix of creamy peanut butter, sweet Thai chilly sauce and fresh ginger.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup sweet Thai chilly sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger

Directions

Step 1
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees(230 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2
Mix ground chicken, egg, bread crumbs, peanut butter, sweet Thai chilly sauce and ginger together in a large bowl.

Step 3
Form the mixture into 1 1/2-inch balls and arrange on the prepared baking sheet.

Step 4
Bake in the preheated oven until browned and no longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer insterted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
